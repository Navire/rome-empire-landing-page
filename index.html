How Roman Legion Works

Legionarius

Legionary was first and foremost a soldier
Professional Heavy Infantryman
Roman citizens under age 45
Equivalent in rank of the modern private

Centurio

Commander of a Century
Centuria: a military unit of around 80 legionaries
Professional Officers
Responsible for the training and discipline of the legionaries under their command

Legatus

High-ranking Roman military officer
Command of a legion.
On Republic: Officially assigned by the Senate
On Empire: The emperor gave the title of legatus legionis to senior commanders
Legatus was also a term for an ambassador of the Roman Republic
